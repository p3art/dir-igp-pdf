Tutorial Eighteen

Drawing Fivefold Pattern in a standard Rectangle

Due to several request, I will illustrate in this tutorial this fivefold pattern. And for the first time I will reveal a very important and significant method of constructing the standard rectangle that encompass most fivefold patterns. I learnt this method from my teacher Mr. Mohamad Abdulrazak Aljanabi. This method shows that the artisans from Baghdad had a very sophisticated geometric knowledge. Their knowledge of geometry is far more advanced than many so called experts. I never saw anyone refer or mention this method up to now. If anyone knows otherwise please let me know.
