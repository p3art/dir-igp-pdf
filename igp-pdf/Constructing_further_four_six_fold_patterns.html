Notes:‐

1. For this patterns you need to construct a six fold rectangle first, please

go to the link below to download the file on “How to construct the six

fold rectangle”.

https://www.facebook.com/groups/zakhrafa.school/permalink/392816221268221/

2. Please do not remove the construction lines when posting your

drawing.

3. The Red lines and circles indicate the present steps.

4. The Orange lines and circles indicate the previous steps.

5. The Red dashed lines and circles indicate extensions of lines and

Arcs.

6. The Red symbol indicates the center location of Circles and Arcs.

7. These two symbols indicate the Radius of Circles and Arcs.
